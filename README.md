Week 2 Assignment Submission 

Part A: RCTTCE Prompt

R – Role
As a Senior Retail Data Analyst with 10+ years of experience in retail performance management, customer analytics, and business strategy.

C – Context
A retail company operates 30 stores and has experienced declining performance over the last 18 months. The CEO has asked the Analytics Team to investigate the causes and identify opportunities for improvement.
The company has provided a dataset containing approximately 50,000 records with the following information:
Store performance
Product sales
Customer transactions
Loyalty programme activity
Key fields include: store_id, transaction_date, product_id, product_name, category, revenue, units_sold, customer_id, loyalty_points, and region.

T – Task
Analyze the business situation and produce a stakeholder brief for the CEO that:
Summarizes the current situation.
Identifies three key business questions that should be investigated.
Explains the business decisions each question will support.
Describes the available data and how it can be used.
F – Format
Present the response using the following headings:
Situation Summary
Key Business Questions
Decisions These Questions Will Inform
Available Data
Keep the brief concise, professional, and suitable for executive stakeholders.

Co – Constraints
Maximum 250 words.
Use clear business language.
Focus on strategic decision-making.
Do not make assumptions that are not supported by the available data.
Present exactly three business questions.
E – Example
Example business question:
"Which stores have experienced the largest decline in revenue and customer transactions over the last 18 months, and what operational or regional factors may explain the decline?"


Part B: AI-Generated Stakeholder Brief
Stakeholder Brief for CEO
Situation Summary
The retail chain has experienced declining performance across its 30 stores over the past 18 months. To understand the causes and identify growth opportunities, an analysis will be conducted using sales, store, product, customer, and loyalty programme data. The objective is to determine whether the decline is driven by specific stores, product categories, or changes in customer purchasing behaviour.

Key Business Questions
Which stores have experienced the greatest decline in revenue and sales volume, and what patterns exist across regions?
Which product categories are driving growth or decline, and how do they impact overall profitability?
Are loyalty programme customers reducing their purchasing frequency, and which customer segments are at risk of churn?
Decisions These Questions Will Inform
Whether to close, restructure, or invest in underperforming stores.
Which products and categories should receive increased marketing, inventory, and promotional support.
Which customer retention strategies should be implemented to improve repeat purchases and loyalty programme engagement.
Available Data
The dataset contains approximately 50,000 records covering store performance, product sales, customer transactions, and loyalty programme activity. Key fields include store identifiers, transaction dates, product details, revenue, units sold, customer identifiers, loyalty points, and regional information. These data elements enable trend analysis, store benchmarking, product performance evaluation, and customer behaviour analysis.
Reflection
I structured the prompt using the RCTTCE framework to provide clear instructions and reduce ambiguity in the AI's response. Defining the role, context, tasks, constraints, and example ensures the output remains focused on executive decision-making while producing a concise and professional stakeholder brief.
